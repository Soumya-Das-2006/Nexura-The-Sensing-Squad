Our Mission

Income Protection for 11 Million Gig Workers

India's delivery workers — powering Zomato, Swiggy, Amazon, Zepto and more — have zero safety net when disruption strikes. A heavy monsoon, a heatwave, a platform outage: any of these can wipe out a day's earnings instantly.

Nexura fixes this with the first fully parametric, AI-powered income protection product designed specifically around how gig workers lose income.

Guidewire DEVTrails 2026

Built at Parul University, Vadodara

Nexura was conceived and built for the Guidewire DEVTrails 2026 hackathon — a challenge to reimagine insurance technology for underserved populations. We chose India's gig delivery workers because the gap between their contribution to the economy and their access to financial protection is massive.

The platform uses trained XGBoost and Prophet ML models, a 6-layer fraud detection pipeline, real-time weather and AQI APIs, and Razorpay's payment infrastructure to deliver a fully automated, parametric income protection product.

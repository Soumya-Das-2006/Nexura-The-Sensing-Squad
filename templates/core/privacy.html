Last updated: March 2026  ·  Nexura, built by Team Nexura, Parul University

1. Information We Collect

When you register with Nexura, we collect:

  • Identity: Name, mobile number, email address
  • KYC: Aadhaar number — stored as a SHA-256 hash only. We never store the raw Aadhaar number.
  • Payment: UPI ID and Razorpay mandate details (no card numbers stored)
  • Location: Your selected delivery zone / city
  • Platform: Your primary delivery platform (Zomato, Swiggy, etc.)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process your policy, claims, and payouts
  • To run the 6-layer fraud detection pipeline
  • To personalise your weekly premium using XGBoost risk scoring
  • To send you claim and payout notifications on WhatsApp
  • To generate your IncomeDNA PDF report

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on AWS (ap-south-1, Mumbai region)
  • Aadhaar numbers are irreversibly hashed using SHA-256 before storage
  • Documents are encrypted and RSA-signed before S3 upload
  • All API endpoints use HTTPS with HSTS in production
  • JWT tokens expire in 15 minutes; refresh tokens in 7 days

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Razorpay — for payment processing and UPI payouts
  • Twilio — for OTP SMS delivery
  • Meta WhatsApp Business API — for notifications
  • AWS — for document storage
  • UIDAI — for Aadhaar OTP verification (production only)

5. Your Rights

  • You may download your IncomeDNA report at any time from your dashboard
  • You may cancel your policy and UPI Autopay mandate from your dashboard
  • You may request deletion of your account by emailing privacy@nexaura.in

6. Contact

For privacy-related queries, contact us at privacy@nexaura.in or via the contact form.
